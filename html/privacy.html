Privacy Policy

Your data is kept safe

InEx Ledger stores the minimum data needed to provide your bookkeeping app. That includes account email, authentication/session information, ledger entries, receipts, exports, and usage signals. The data may be processed and stored in the United States and is governed by U.S. law while remaining compliant with PIPEDA transparency expectations for Canadian users.

Data we collect

  • Account credentials and email
  • Auth/session tokens
  • App usage and navigation data
  • Financial entries you enter (transactions, receipts, recurring data)
  • Exports and reports you generate

Data we do not sell

We do not sell your personal data. That means no targeted advertising or profiling for sale. Your data stays within InEx Ledger unless you explicitly export it yourself.

Your rights

New Jersey data privacy law (effective Jan 15, 2025) guarantees:

  • Access and correction of your data
  • Deletion and portability of your records
  • Opt-out of targeted advertising, sale, or profiling

How to exercise rights

Use Settings > Privacy & Data to change your preferences, download data, or request deletion. For manual requests, email support@lunabusiness.com.

Retention

Account and ledger data is retained until a deletion request is honored. Business data may remain in backups only as required by law.

Security

Passwords are hashed before storage, TLS encrypts every request, and access follows least-privilege principles. We monitor for suspicious behavior to keep your records safe.

Canada GST/HST note

If you are in Canada and your total taxable revenue exceeds $30,000 CAD within twelve months, you may have obligations to register and collect GST/HST. Use InEx Ledger to keep clean records, and consult your advisor for the next steps.
